1. Parties and Purpose

This contract is entered into between the Texas Health and Human Services Commission (hereinafter referred to as "the Agency") and Lone Star Medical Transport (hereinafter referred to as "the Provider") for the purpose of providing non-emergency medical transportation services to eligible recipients in the designated service areas.

2. Definitions

Definitions of key terms used in this contract will be provided in this section.

3. Term and Renewal

This contract shall be effective for a period of three (3) years from the contract date, with the option to renew for two (2) additional one-year periods upon mutual agreement of both parties.

4. Scope of Services

The Provider shall provide non-emergency medical transportation services to eligible recipients in the following counties: Harris County, Fort Bend County, and Montgomery County.

5. Provider Responsibilities

The Provider shall maintain a fleet of at least 75 vehicles and employ a minimum of 180 drivers to ensure 24/7 operations. The Provider shall ensure that all drivers are properly licensed, trained, and comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

6. Agency Responsibilities

The Agency shall be responsible for determining recipient eligibility, authorizing transportation services, and providing necessary information to the Provider for the coordination of services.

8. Compliance and Reporting

The Provider shall comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and policies. The Provider shall also submit regular reports to the Agency regarding service utilization, performance metrics, and any other required information.
